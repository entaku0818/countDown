Frequently Asked Questions

How many events can I register in the free version?

The free version allows you to register up to 3 events. A premium version is planned for the future.

When will I receive notifications?

By default, you'll receive notifications the day before your event. You can adjust this in the notification settings.

What iOS versions are supported?

The app supports iOS 17.0 and later.
